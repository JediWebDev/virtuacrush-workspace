This Privacy Statement describes how **[The Company]** ("Virtua Crush," "we," or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you use the Virtua Crush website at **[VIRTUACRUSH.COM]** and any associated applications (the "Services").

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**Account information.** When you register, we collect your email address, display name, and authentication credentials.

**Conversation content.** Messages you exchange with AI companions are processed and stored to provide the Services, including memory and personalization features.

**Payment information.** Subscription payments are processed by our payment provider (Stripe). We do not store full card numbers.

**Usage data.** We collect device, browser, and interaction data to operate, secure, and improve the Services.

## 2. How We Use Information

We use information to provide and personalize the Services, process payments, communicate with you, enforce our Terms of Service and Acceptable Use Policy, and comply with legal obligations.

## 3. AI Processing

Conversation content may be processed by third-party AI infrastructure providers to generate companion responses. These providers process content on our behalf under contractual restrictions.

## 4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only with service providers who support the Services, when required by law, or in connection with a business transfer.

## 5. Retention and Deletion

We retain information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Services. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting **[SUPPORT@VIRTUACRUSH.COM]**.

## 6. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal information, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact **[SUPPORT@VIRTUACRUSH.COM]**.

## 7. Age Requirement

The Services are restricted to users 18 years of age or older. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If we learn that a minor has provided us information, we will delete it and terminate the account.

## 8. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 9. Changes to This Statement

We may update this Privacy Statement from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated statement and revising the "Last Updated" date.

## 10. Contact

Questions about this Privacy Statement may be directed to **[SUPPORT@VIRTUACRUSH.COM]**.
