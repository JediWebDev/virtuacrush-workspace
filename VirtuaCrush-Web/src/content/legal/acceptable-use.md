## 1. Eligibility

You must be at least 18 years of age, or the age of majority in your jurisdiction, to use the Services.

## 2. Prohibited Content

You may not submit, generate, or attempt to generate content that:

- involves, depicts, or sexualizes minors in any way;
- depicts or promotes non-consensual sexual activity;
- constitutes hate speech or incites violence against any person or group;
- promotes self-harm, suicide, or eating disorders;
- promotes terrorism, violent extremism, or mass violence;
- facilitates illegal activity, including the sale of controlled substances or weapons;
- infringes the intellectual property or privacy rights of others; or
- impersonates a real person without their consent.

## 3. Prohibited Conduct

You may not:

- attempt to bypass safety systems, content filters, or usage limits;
- use automated tools to access the Services without authorization;
- probe, scan, or test the vulnerability of our systems;
- resell, redistribute, or commercially exploit the Services or generated content without authorization; or
- harass, threaten, or abuse our staff or other users.

## 4. Enforcement

We may remove content, suspend, or terminate accounts that violate this Policy, at our sole discretion and without prior notice. We may report unlawful content to relevant authorities where required or permitted by law.

## 5. Reporting

To report content or conduct that violates this Policy, contact **[SUPPORT@VIRTUACRUSH.COM]**.
